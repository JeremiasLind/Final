The Ray gun

This hand held ray-gun is a innovation in the recent decade. This tech allows people to measure the speed of objects such as balls which are flying through the air. This makes it easier for football players and baseball players to see if they are improving their skills. Now these athletes can measure their skill and prove it with recorded data. This device also comes in different modes, including non hand-held versions.

The Halo

The f1 Halo is the most important of the new innovations on this list. It is a safety measure for formula one race drivers. It is named after the angelic halo ring due to its looks and functions Its only purpose is to protect the head of the driver in the case that the vehicle is going to flip.
